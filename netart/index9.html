Ghostly Touch

I could tell you needed a hug.
not from me.
or anyone else living
to my friend mourning I say,
I hope her ghost finds ways to touch you
Through almost forgotten memories(s)
Speaks to you through lyrics from songs
Through pages that you and she had turned together
I pray her ghost visits in places that only she could enter
May her absence embrace you better than I could.-Terry
